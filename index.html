How it works

Three steps. Zero coordination.

No messaging. No scheduling. No haggling. Just put it on the curb and let Kurbee do the rest.

1
📸

Post your item

Take a photo, pick a category, and confirm your address. Your listing goes live on the map in under 60 seconds.

2
🗺️

Browse nearby

Pickers explore a live map of available items in their neighborhood. Filter by category, tap a pin, see the photo and details.

3
✅

Pick it up

Drive or walk to the item. When you're within 500 feet, the pickup button unlocks. Tap to confirm — the item is yours.

Trust & Safety

Built for your neighborhood.

Kurbee is designed to keep your community safe and the platform trustworthy.

📍

GPS-verified pickup

Pickers must physically be within 500 feet of the item to confirm pickup. No false claims.

⏰

Auto-expiry

Listings automatically expire the morning after your trash collection day — creating urgency and keeping the map clean.

🚩

Report button

Every listing has a report button. Our team reviews flagged content and removes anything that violates our guidelines.

🔒

Accounts required

Both posters and pickers need an account. This keeps the community accountable and reduces misuse.
